Z-Pattern
01
Z-Pattern: Perfect for Landing Pages
The Z-pattern is ideal for pages with less text and more visual content, such as landing pages or home pages. Eyes follow a “Z” shape: starting at the top left, moving to the right, then diagonally to the bottom left, and then to the right again.
02
Z-Pattern: Perfect for Landing Pages
Top Bar: Like the F-pattern, begin with crucial elements like the logo and navigation at the top. Visuals: Use compelling images or graphics across the top and middle to guide the eyes from left to right. Call to Action (CTA): Place a strong CTA at the final point of the Z, usually at the bottom right.
